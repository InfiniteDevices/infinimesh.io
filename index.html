What is the infinimesh IoT Platform?

We believe that smart and connected devices advance our society. Smart technology uses resources only when they are truly required, reducing waste. Smart things, on the other hand, act when necessary, preventing accidents or simply enhancing the user experience. We began infinimesh as a 100% open source project, with no patents or closed software. Any software components we develop, and to which we commit in the future, will be open - forever. Infinimesh's mission is to make industrial and personal IoT secure, accessible, and affordable to all.


Status and features

infinimesh provides you with a state-of-the-art backbone for your IoT fleet:

  • High Scalability: Infinimesh can handle millions of devices while maintaining peak performance.
  • Highest Security: RSA-proven encryption technology fully protects IoT connectivity and data transfers.
  • Industry proven: Plugins for certain enterprise backends, incl. BACnet MQTT Gateway
  • Complete openness: We collaborate with the community to produce the finest stack possible and our code is Open Source.
  • Maximum Usability: Infinimesh is ready to use in scenarios using Docker and Kubernetes.

    • IoT has never been simpler thanks to infinimesh, the first open source IoT platform that use Cloud Native Kubernetes.



    Infinimesh is based on two main components.

    Asset Management

    Infinimesh provides quick and deep insights into all IoT assets managed - whether they are managed through gateways, edge devices, or directly connected - so you never lose track of what your fleet is doing!
    Scaled IoT and IIoT operations necessitate transparency and insights into the managed fleet. Infinimesh has created a graph-based organizing framework with real-time device statistics. Arrange your devices in an overview that reflects your organizational or project-based setup, manage which devices belong where, manage groups on different layers, and define who has access using the RBAC model. To ensure complete security, we offer granular access management down to the device level. IoT operations have never been simpler!

    IoT Security

    Building new IoT or IIoT products also necessitates the implementation of manageable security mechanisms. Knowing which device was built with the correct certificate is required for strategic implementations beginning with factory provisioning. The most important factor in a successful IoT business chain is trust. Learn about the infinimesh IoT Platform and how it can help you build a trusted fleet of devices. We use military-grade encryption standards to ensure secure message transmission even over unreliable and unstable WAN connections such as LoRa, SigFox, WhiteNoise, or Satellite. Our MQTT broker accepts only certificates known to the signed CA and ensures that unknown but trusted devices can send data into a dedicated secure queue - never lose any information during a long rollout.



    With leading industry features - built-in

    Device Management

    Powerful but simple framework to visualize clusters of devices within your organization and set permissions up to device level

    Device Shadow

    Real-time and two-way correspondence for every device in your fleet. Our highly-scalable backend can power millions of devices

    Asset Monitoring

    Some statistics about your device fleet at your chosen level of abstractions. Detect problems quickly and at a glance

    Timeseries Visualization

    Great telemetry is based on timeseries. infinimesh has timeseries data capabilities built-in and enables meaningful monitoring

    Virtual Twins

    A virtual twin is the digital copy of your physical asset. infinimesh provides virtual twins which give you the possibility to modify your physical device without even touching it

    Cloud Native

    infinimesh is portable Cloud Native Software - we leverage the most modern approach to make the setup, operation and relocation of a fleet or even a complete installation easy and reliable. This was never done before

    Hybrid Cloud

    Infinimesh has the unique capability of running in multiple locations at the same time. This eliminates complex and vulnerable installations and frees up space to build instead to debug.

    Discover the potential

    Electric grids

    The future energy transition will be supported by smart grids and intelligent infrastructure. Infinimesh makes your long-term assets smart without increasing their cost or complexity. Monitoring low-voltage grids, managing high-voltage transformers, and performing complex switch scenarios are all areas where the platform excels, thanks to the team's extensive experience in utility and energy distribution companies.

    Renewables

    Operating renewable energy systems such as windmills, solar farms, biomass, or combined heat and power assets generate a large amount of telemetry data, which can be used to create new business models by combining it with ML and AI technology to gain better insights into maintenance and profit optimization. To make use of the generated data, a new operating and management model is required. Infinimesh enables true data-driven business models and provides confidence in managing large and expensive assets in a cost-effective and reliable manner.

    Automotive

    Infinimesh can power connected and self-driving cars, advanced robots, autonomous drones, smart cities, and interconnected railroads. We address the most pressing issue in this automated and interconnected world: reliable and secure connectivity. Real-time (RTS) data processing in complex networks is enabled by the upcoming communication revolution (5G, IIoT, NFRB). Infinimesh is the workhorse for everything you want to build because it is designed for low latency, data QoS, and high data ingest.

    Industry

    Monitoring and storing telemetry data from large production lines spread across thousands of miles across a continent presents challenges for predictive maintenance, worker safety tracking, and geological irregulation detection. Even with disrupted data connection lines such as LTE, 3G, LoRa, or SigFox, infinimesh enables fractional and trouble-free data transmission and can handle time series even when the transmission date is unknown, allowing an entire industry to become truly digitally driven.

    Install and Setup


    It takes less than a minute to install infinimesh IoT. The Kubernetes Operator manages maintenance and updates, saving valuable time in operations and maintenance. Infinimesh integrates seamlessly into your software development landscape, whether you are building smart products or leveraging IoT devices to improve your processes. Use our managed cloud service or install infinimesh on your own server. Even without Kubernetes, infinimesh runs everywhere.
